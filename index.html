Cultural Heritage

Rich History & Culture

Explore the diverse cultural heritage of Marathwada, from ancient caves to medieval fortresses, and vibrant local traditions.

Lavani Dance

A traditional folk dance celebrated for its powerful rhythm and expressive movements, often performed during festivals.

Ganesh Chaturthi

The grand festival honoring Lord Ganesha, marked by vibrant processions, music, and community celebrations across Maharashtra.

Gudi Padwa

The Maharashtrian New Year, celebrated with vibrant processions, traditional foods, and the raising of the Gudi flag for prosperity.

Makar Sankranti

A harvest festival marked by kite flying, sharing of tilgul sweets, and community gatherings to welcome the sun's northward journey.

Nag Panchami

A unique festival where serpents are worshipped, reflecting Maharashtra's deep-rooted traditions and reverence for nature.

Warli Art

Ancient tribal art form using geometric patterns to depict daily life, nature, and rituals, originating from the Warli tribe.
